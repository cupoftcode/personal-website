About
From beauty entrepreneur to marketer & founder.

I ran a lash and beauty business from 19 to 24, then made the leap into tech and AI. Today I'm a solo founder building at the intersection of travel compliance, AI, and community.

My background is in marketing and community building. I'm self-taught on the technical side, going from no-code tools to a full local dev workflow with React, Supabase, and Claude Code. I describe myself as "technically mid" but I ship fast and learn faster.

I'm deeply invested in making AI builder spaces more inclusive, especially for women and neurodivergent builders. I believe the best products come from communities that actually reflect the people using them.
